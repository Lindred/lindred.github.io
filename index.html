Lindred is an experimental musician currently based between the US and Berlin, Germany.

Twisted holy hymns: underworld angel vocals that wind through sonic falls from grace. Their work orbits around devotional sound, altered states, and modern intimacy. As an artist, they have composed for various mediums spanning film, dance-theater, and installations.
